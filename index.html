Yang Liu (刘阳)

Research Associate Professor
IEEE Member, IEEE Signal Processing Society Member
Email: liuy856@mail.sysu.edu.cn; aryanr@163.com; aryanr0802@gmail.com
Office: School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun-Yat-sen University, Guangzhou, China

I am currently a Research Associate Professor at HCP Lab, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, working with Prof. Liang Lin. From 2019-2021, I worked as a posdoctoral fellow at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University. I obtained my Ph.D degree of Telecommunications and Information Systems in June 2019 at Phoenix-eye (XD145) Lab, School of Telecommunications Engineering, Xidian University, advised by Prof. Zhaoyang Lu and Prof. Jing Li. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in telecommunications engineering, School of Information Engineering, Chang’an University in 2014. I graduated from ZhiXin High School in 2010. I have authored and coauthored more than 15 papers in top-tier academic journals and conferences including IEEE T-IP (3 papers), IEEE T-II, IEEE T-CSVT, IEEE SPL and CVPR. I serve as the reviewer of CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, PRCV, IEEE T-IP, T-NNLS, IEEE T-CYB, IEEE T-MM, IEEE T-CSVT, IEEE T-HMS, IEEE SPL, PR, and NSFC, etc. I am hosting several key national projects including NSFC, NSFC Guangdong, and Chinese Postdoctoral Foundation. I am the member of IEEE and IEEE Signal Processing Society. My research interests include:

  • Computer Vision: Image/Video Analysis and Understanding, Action Recognition, Visual-Linguistic Reasoning, Anomaly Detection
  • Machine Learning: Causal Reasoning, Transfer Learning, Domain Adaptation
  • Representation Learning: Deep Learning, Multi-modal Learning, Self-supervised Learning, Attention/Transformer/Big Models
  • Urban Computing: Spatial-temporal Anomaly Detection, Traffic Elements Prediction, Data Mining
My representative first-author papers are:
  • TCGL: Temporal Contrastive Graph for Self-supervised Video Representation Learning, IEEE Transactions on Image Processing, 2022
  • Semantics-aware Adaptive Knowledge Distillation for Sensor-to-Vision Action Recognition, IEEE Transactions on Image Processing, 2021
  • Deep Image-to-Video Adaptation and Fusion Networks for Action Recognition, IEEE Transactions on Image Processing, 2020
  • Hierarchically Learned View-Invariant Representations for Cross View Action Recognition, IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, 2019
  • Global Temporal Representation based CNNs for Infrared Action Recognition, IEEE Signal Processing Letters, 2018
